The Sound

Rich jazz harmony with a funky groove. Counter-culture, class-conscious hip-hop lyricism. Desires and emotions made manifest through sound. We have an obligation to articulate a moral vision derived from the unconscious.

Our reality is an expression of the cosmic consensus: that humankind is good, and all are equal. The power of music to inspire others and create community cannot be overstated — we can cut through the miasma of today’s isolationist, hyper-capitalist existence and catalyze meaningful social change.

About the type

This website is set in Linux Libertine, a typeface originally designed by Philipp H. Poll. Inspired by 19th century book type, this baroque serif conveys comfort and poise. The accompanying sans-serif, Linux Biolinum, features subtle stroke modulation for an organic feel with a touch of elegance.

You may download both families for free at the Libertine Fonts Project website. These fonts are packaged under the SIL Open Font License and may be used for commercial and non-commercial purposes. Remember, Type is a political choice.
